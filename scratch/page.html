  • The Standard of Luxury on Ishigaki： This is the reliable, classic accommodation that first comes to mind when talking about resorts on Ishigaki. RitoTabi Expert Review
In a superb location with Kabira Area’s scenic Sukuji Beach spreading right in front. Ideal for those who want to feel Ishigaki’s rich nature firsthand in a quiet environment.
Check!What I loved about my stay
  • An overwhelmingly immersive Private Resort： With no other buildings around, it feels like being thrown into Ishigaki’s nature in a good way. It’s the ultimate luxury environment for those who want to enjoy the hotel stay quietly or take a nap with the sound of waves as a lullaby.
  • A rare authentic hotel in the Kabira area： If you seek hotel-like facilities and service in this area, your choices are here or Club Med. It stands apart from pensions and guest houses, offering solid hospitality.
  • A vast garden where children can play freely： With a lawn area wide enough for soccer, a pool, and a private beach, it has the potential for families to enjoy themselves within the hotel all day.
  • The gentle sea directly connected to Sukuji Beach： The private beach is connected to the scenic Sukuji Beach, and the waves are very gentle. Even those with small children can safely let them have their sea debut.
  • Stocking up in advance is a matter of life and death： There are no supermarkets or convenience stores nearby. It is an absolute condition for a comfortable stay to stop by a supermarket in the city before check-in and stock up on drinks and snacks.
  • Stress-free, spacious parking： There is a vast parking space in front of the hotel, so even those unconfident in driving rental cars or parking can use it with peace of mind.
  • Heaven in fair weather, a fortress in rain： While the location is wonderful, it’s inconvenient to go anywhere in bad weather. Don’t forget to prepare books or gadgets for staying in.

Runner's Review:Jogging is mainly on the single road round-trip along the island’s edge. While points with good views are limited, the charm of running through quintessentially Okinawan sugarcane fields more than makes up for it.

05 FAQ

Q

Which is the most luxurious hotel in Ishigaki?

A
ANA InterContinental Ishigaki Resort By IHG is the premier luxury destination, offering extensive facilities and world-class service.
Q

Which Ishigaki hotel has the best breakfast?

A
The BREAKFAST HOTEL PORTO Ishigakijima is a standout choice for its lavish breakfast buffet — think Ishigaki beef roast beef, fresh seafood, and flavors that will send you back for seconds.
Q

Which hotel is recommended for families with children?

A
Fusaki Beach Resort Hotel & Villas is highly recommended. It stands right on a natural beach with extensive pool facilities, and its villa-style rooms offer a spacious stay for families.
Q

Which hotel is most convenient for the ferry terminal?

A
Hotel Miyahira is located directly in front of the Ishigaki Port Ferry Terminal, making it the perfect base for island hopping to Taketomi, Iriomote, and other islands.
Q

Where should I stay for a quiet, nature-focused environment?

A
Ishigaki Seaside Hotel is ideal. Directly connected to Sukuji Beach in the Kabira area, it offers a peaceful resort experience away from the city’s bustle.
Q

Which hotel is the best base for running?

A
Hotel Miyahira and The BREAKFAST HOTEL PORTO Ishigakijima are ideal bases, offering excellent access to major routes like the Southern Gate Bridge and the 730 Monument.
