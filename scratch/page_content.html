  • Grocery Caution： There are no shops or convenience stores on Kurima Island. Buy everything you need at a supermarket on the Miyako mainland before crossing the bridge.

05 FAQ

Q

Which is the most luxurious hotel in Miyako Island?

A
IRAPH SUI, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Miyako Okinawa, is the top choice. As a Marriott Luxury Collection brand, it offers ultimate serenity and breathtaking views for an adult retreat.
Q

Which hotel is best for families with children?

A
Miyakojima Tokyu Hotel & Resorts is highly recommended. Set directly on the world-class Yonaha Maehama Beach, it features excellent pools and family-friendly services.
Q

Are there convenient hotels near city centers?

A
Hotel Risingsun Miyakojima and HOTEL LOCUS offer great access to the city center for dining and shopping. Risingsun is particularly convenient as it's located right across from AEON Town shopping mall.
Q

Where can I buy groceries near IRAPH SUI?

A
The supermarket on the 1st floor of Hotel South Island at the entrance of Irabu Island is very convenient. It's just a few minutes drive away and perfect for stocking up on snacks and drinks.
Q

What should I be careful about when staying at Shigira Resort?

A
Dining options outside the resort are very limited. We strongly recommend booking resort restaurants in advance to avoid being stranded for dinner during peak seasons.
Q

Which hotel is best for runners?

A
Hilton Okinawa Miyako Island Resort, which connects directly to a scenic sunset beach route, and HOTEL LOCUS, convenient for city runs through Hirara's historical streets, are both excellent choices.
Q

Which hotel is closest to the airport?

A
HOTEL TORIFITO MIYAKOJIMA RESORT is located right in front of Miyako Airport, making it incredibly accessible for those landing late or departing early.
